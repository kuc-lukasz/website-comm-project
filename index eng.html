YOU OWN THE LAND? - LET'S TALK HOW WE CAN HELP YOU

Are you the land owner? Are you wondering whether to lease it under building a solar farm? Contact us. We will make sure that you achieve consistent high profits without the need for commitment own work or funds and investment.

    What requirements must your plot meet?

  • width from east to west, min. 60 m
  • access to a public road
  • land class IV, V and VI
  • area not included in a landscape or national park

  • High lease rent payable for each hectare used for solar power plant plus property tax payment
  • Guarantee of long-term, reliable income - contract for 29 years
  • Annual rent indexation by the inflation rate
  • Favorable terms and a clear lease agreement
  • Work related to the investment entirely on the part of the investor
  • All costs related to the investment are on our side
  • Restoration of the land to its original condition after completion of the investment
